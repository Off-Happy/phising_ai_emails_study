## Welcome

Thank you for taking part in this study.

This study examines how people make sense of **everyday emails** and decide how to respond to them, in a context where advances in **large language models (LLMs)** are increasingly shaping how such emails are created.

## What is an “LLM”?

A **large language model (LLM)** is a type of **AI** that can **generate or revise text** based on a prompt. Tools such as **ChatGPT** are examples of systems based on LLMs.

## What you will do

In this study, you will:

- Answer a small number of **background questions**.
- Review **six emails**, presented one at a time, and provide your responses (with an **optional short explanation**).
- Complete a brief set of **follow-up questions** and, if you wish, leave **general feedback**.

Please note that once you **submit** a response to a question, you will **not** be able to go back and change it.

## Time and device

The survey takes approximately **10–15 minutes** to complete, although this may vary.

For the best experience, we recommend using a **laptop or desktop computer**. If you are using a **mobile device**, rotating it to **landscape mode** may help.

## After you finish

After completing the survey, you will see a brief summary of your responses.

## Your data and participation

- Your participation is **voluntary**.
- Your responses will be used for **research purposes** and analyzed in **aggregate**.
- Please do **not** include **identifying or personal information** in any open-ended responses.
- You may **stop participating at any time**. If you stop before completing the survey, any responses stored on this device will be **deleted**.

By continuing with the survey, you indicate that you **understand** the information above and **agree to participate**.
