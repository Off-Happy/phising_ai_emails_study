## Welcome
Thank you for taking part in our phishing email perception study.

## Purpose
We are investigating how wording and possible AI generation influence how legitimate an email appears. Your answers help improve phishing detection and user safety.

## What You Will Do
1. Review a series of emails (each shown once).
2. For each email, inspect the content and (optionally) its headers.
3. Answer a few short questions about phishing suspicion and possible AI involvement.
