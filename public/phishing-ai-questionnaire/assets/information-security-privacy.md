## Security & privacy questions

**Recommendation (mobile):** If you’re on a phone, rotating to **landscape** can make the questions easier to read.

### How to answer

- Please answer based on your **usual behaviour**, not only what you did during this study.

There are no right or wrong answers—please respond honestly.
