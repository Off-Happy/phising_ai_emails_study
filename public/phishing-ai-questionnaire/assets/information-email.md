## How to answer the email pages

**Recommendation (mobile):** If you’re on a phone, rotating to **landscape** can make the email easier to read.

On the next pages, you’ll be shown a series of emails.

### What to do

- Read each email carefully and imagine it arrived in your own inbox.
- Use your best judgment based on what you notice in the message.

### How to answer

Below the email you’ll answer a few **statements** on a **1–5 scale** (Strongly disagree → Strongly agree). Indicate how much you agree or disagree with each statement.

If the email isn’t fully visible, scroll down to view the entire message.
