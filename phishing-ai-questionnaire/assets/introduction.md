## Welcome
Thank you for taking part in this short survey.

This study looks at how people interpret and respond to everyday emails. Some emails you see may have been written or edited with assistance from modern text-generation tools.

**What is an “LLM”?** An **LLM (Large Language Model)** is a type of **AI** that can generate or rewrite text based on a prompt. You may have heard of tools like **ChatGPT** these are examples of LLM-based systems.

## What you will do
- Answer a few **background questions**.
- Review **6 emails**, one at a time, and provide your ratings (with an optional short explanation).
- Answer a short set of follow-up questions and optionally leave feedback.

## Time and device
This survey typically takes about **10–15 minutes** (some people may finish faster or slower).

For the best experience, use a laptop or desktop. If you use a phone, rotating to landscape may help.

## After you finish
At the end of the survey, you will see a brief summary of your responses.

## Your data
- Participation is voluntary.
- Your responses are recorded for research purposes and analyzed in aggregate.
- Please avoid entering identifying information in any open text fields.
- You may stop at any time. If you stop before finishing, any responses stored on this device will be deleted.
